PERSONAL PENSION PLAN


Personal Pension Plan is a personal financial plan that helps the holder to accumulate
fund towards the provision of retirement income.

Who can purchase Personal Pension Plan ?


* Self-employed individuals who are not members of social security fund

* Employees or directors who are members of social security fund but desire to
augment their retirement income

* Employers for the benefit of their employees







Benefits and features of Personal Pension Plan


  • Individual policies are issued

  • Flexible payment of contributions (yearly, half-yearly, quarterly, monthly)

  • Optional Life Insurance benefit equals to 10 x annual premium.

  • Annual Compound interest of 4%

  • A surrender value is payable after at least 3 years regular contributions while
    a partial surrender equals to maximum 70% is also payable.

  • Waiver of contributions in case of death or total permanent disability (in this
    case the beneficiaries benefit total life cover + surrender value)

  • It continues irrespective of change of jobs

  • Individual statement of fund balances is provided

  • All benefits are tax free

  • Policy serves as a security for loans (with Life cover)
