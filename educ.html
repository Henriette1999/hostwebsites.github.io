Eduction Endowment


It’s an endowment policy subscribed by the policyholder (parent or guardian of the child) to cater for the Child’s education in future.

Policyholder chooses the basic maturity value or premium to be paid regularly (monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or yearly) and premiumsare paid during the premium payment period for the education
level assured (Ordinary, advanced and tertiary levels with different policies for each of them).

This investment linked life assurance policy was designed for individuals who want maximum protection for their beneficiaries in the event of the Life assureds’ death.








Features:


  • Premium payable is to cover life and investment according to the individual age.

  • Transferable to another beneficiary (child) if the policyholder chooses so.

    • Benefits:


    In case of death: payment of 50% of annual education benefits on the contract until end of the
    differed period of premium payment then after full payment of annual benefits until end of the
    education level assured.

  • If life assured survives at the end of the period of premium payment: The Company pays
    annual benefits until end of the level assured.

  • Waiver of premium in case of death or total permanent disability of the assured

  • Child income benefit

  • Benefits are tax free
