SONARWA Life

SONARWA Life Assurance Company Limited ( SONARWA LIFE ) is a public
Limited Liability company registered under the laws of Rwanda and licensed by
the National Bank of Rwanda to transact the business of Life Assurance and
Pension management. The company started in 2000 as a division under
SONARWA S.A. (now SONARWA General) and became a full-fledged registered
entity in January 2011. It is currently 100% wholly owned by the Rwanda
Social Security Board (RSSB)
